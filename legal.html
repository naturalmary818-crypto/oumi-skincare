Returns & Refund Policy

We want you to love your OUMI products. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Items must be unused, unopened and in original packaging
  • Opened or used products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons, unless the item is faulty or damaged upon arrival

How to Return

  • All purchases are made via our Shopee Malaysia store
  • To initiate a return, open a return request directly via your Shopee order page
  • Returns are processed in accordance with Shopee Malaysia's buyer protection policy

Refunds

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days once the return is received and inspected
  • Refunds are returned to your original payment method via Shopee

Damaged or Faulty Products
